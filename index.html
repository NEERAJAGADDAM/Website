Telangana Social Welfare Residential Educational Institutions Society (TSWREIS) is dedicated to providing quality education to the underprivileged children in the state of Telangana. Our schools and junior colleges are designed to offer a holistic education that focuses not just on academics but also on overall personality development.



At TSWREIS, we believe in nurturing talent and potential through a supportive and challenging learning environment. Our students are encouraged to excel in their studies, participate in extracurricular activities, and develop skills that will help them succeed in life.

With a dedicated team of educators and state-of-the-art facilities, TSWREIS ensures that every student receives the attention and guidance they need to reach their full potential. We are committed to transforming lives through education and empowering the next generation of leaders.
